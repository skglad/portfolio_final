Some of our favorite spots in Madrid:

"Gran Via"

Gran Via is known as the "broadway of Madrid" with lots of shopping, shows, and restaurants. It's always busy and always fun!

"La Latina"

The first neighborhood that Sara lived in and a very "Madrileño" neighbor. Great for tapas and cervezas on Sunday afternoons.

"The Oso"

The Oso (the bear) is located in the Puerta del Sol, the city center of Madrid - filled with people and action at all hours of the day.

Places to visit around Spain:

"Barcelona"

Barcelona, of course, is a popular destination within Spain. The city is very different than Madrid.

"La Rioja"

La Rioja is a wine region in the north of Spain. It's close enough to Madrid to take a quick day trip out to some wineries.

"Cordoba"

Cordoba is a small city in the south of Spain with tons of culture, good food, and great weather (year round!).

Spanish Islands we love:

"Ibiza"

Ibiza is known as the party destination of Europe but it also has beautiful beaches and coastlines for a relaxing vacation.

"Tenerife"

Tenerife is one of the Canary Islands, and home to the highest peak in Spain on Mount Teide. The island is worth a visit any time of the year.

"Formentera"

Formentera is the quietest of the Baleares Islands. With amazing beaches and beautiful sceneray, it's a great place to relax.
